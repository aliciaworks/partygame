## FAQ

**Q: Can I use PartyGame in my commercial game without a commercial license?**

A: No. The AGPL-3.0 copyleft clause requires you to either:
1. Release your modifications under AGPL-3.0 (which may not be acceptable for proprietary games)
2. Obtain a commercial license

**Q: What does the "network copyleft" clause mean?**

A: The AGPL-3.0 includes provisions for network server software. If you modify PartyGame and run it as a service, you must make the modified source code available to users. This is stronger than standard GPL for server-side code.

**Q: Can I get a commercial license for just parts of PartyGame?**

A: Commercial license terms are negotiable. Contact us to discuss your specific needs.

**Q: Is there a free tier for startups?**

A: We may offer free or discounted commercial licenses for qualifying startups. Please reach out to discuss your situation.
